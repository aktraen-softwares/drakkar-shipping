About Drakkar Shipping

Connecting the Nordics to the world since 1987.

Our Story

Drakkar Shipping was founded in 1987 by Bjorn Lindqvist in Stavanger, starting with a single cargo vessel running freight between the Nordics and the rest of Europe. Over nearly four decades, we have grown into the Nordics' leading independent freight logistics company.

Today, we operate a fleet of 8 vessels and manage air cargo partnerships through major Nordic airports, connecting Nordic businesses to over 40 European destinations. Our team of 180 employees handles everything from maritime freight and cold chain logistics to customs brokerage and last-mile delivery.
