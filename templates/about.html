About Drakkar Shipping

Connecting Iceland to the world since 1987.

Our Story

Drakkar Shipping was founded in 1987 by Bjorn Thorvaldsson in Hafnarfjordur, starting with a single cargo vessel running freight between Iceland and Norway. Over nearly four decades, we have grown into Iceland's leading independent freight logistics company.

Today, we operate a fleet of 8 vessels and manage air cargo partnerships through Keflavik Airport, connecting Icelandic businesses to over 40 European destinations. Our team of 180 employees handles everything from maritime freight and cold chain logistics to customs brokerage and last-mile delivery.
