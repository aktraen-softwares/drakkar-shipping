Our Services

Comprehensive shipping and logistics solutions tailored for North Atlantic trade routes.

⚓ Maritime Freight

Our core service connects Icelandic ports with major European hubs. We operate weekly scheduled sailings from Reykjavik, Akureyri, and Isafjordur.

  • Full Container Load (FCL) — 20ft and 40ft containers
  • Less than Container Load (LCL) — consolidated shipments
  • Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) for vehicles and heavy machinery
  • Project cargo and oversized freight

Destinations: Rotterdam, Hamburg, London, Bergen, Copenhagen, Oslo, Dublin

✈ Air Cargo

For time-critical shipments, our air cargo service offers express delivery through Keflavik International Airport.

  • Next-day delivery to major European cities
  • Charter flights for urgent or oversized cargo
  • Door-to-door service with ground transport coordination
  • Dangerous goods handling (IATA certified)

📦 Cold Chain Logistics

Iceland's fishing and pharmaceutical industries require precision temperature control. Our cold chain infrastructure maintains product integrity from origin to destination.

  • Reefer containers: -25°C to +25°C
  • Real-time temperature monitoring and logging
  • HACCP and GDP compliant processes
  • Dedicated cold storage at Reykjavik terminal

📄 Customs & Documentation

Navigating customs between Iceland and the EU requires expertise. Our licensed brokers handle all documentation and clearance.

  • Import and export customs declarations
  • AEO certified operator — expedited processing
  • Veterinary and phytosanitary certificates for food products
  • Trade compliance consulting

🚚 Domestic Distribution

Last-mile delivery across Iceland, from Reykjavik metro to remote Westfjords communities.

  • Scheduled routes to all major towns
  • Express same-day delivery in greater Reykjavik
  • Warehouse and distribution centre in Hafnarfjordur
